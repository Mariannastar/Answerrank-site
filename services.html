Do I need the audit before starting a retainer?

Not necessarily, but it's the smartest starting point. The audit tells us exactly what's broken before we start building. If you go straight to a retainer, we run a lighter audit in month one as part of onboarding. Either way, we start with a clear picture of where you stand.

What results can I realistically expect?

First AI citations on Perplexity and ChatGPT typically appear within 2-4 weeks of publishing well-optimised content. Google AI Overview citations usually take 2-3 months. Meaningful increases in AI referral traffic within 3-6 months. Google rankings follow the standard 3-6 month SEO timeline. We track all of this and report on it monthly.

Can I start with the content strategy and execute it myself?

Yes. The content strategy gives you a complete, ready-to-execute roadmap: every keyword, every brief, every piece of schema. Some founders execute it in-house. Others hand it to a writer. Others come back to us for the retainer once they see the strategy. There's no obligation to continue after the strategy is delivered.

What makes AnswerRank different from an SEO agency?

A standard SEO agency optimises for Google rankings. That's the game they were built for. AnswerRank was built for the game as it exists now, where your potential clients are asking ChatGPT for recommendations and getting AI-generated answers on Google before they see a single organic result. We optimise for Google and AI search at once using one integrated workflow. That's not something most agencies offer because most haven't built it yet.
