Before you request your audit

Is the free audit really free?

Yes. No credit card required. No automatic sign-up. We review every request personally and deliver your summary by email within 48 hours.

What's the difference between the free and paid audit?

The free audit reviews your top 3 pages and gives you 3 quick wins, enough to understand where you stand and start fixing things. The paid audit reviews your top 10 pages with a complete PDF report, schema analysis, competitor benchmarking, topical gap analysis, and a 20-minute walkthrough call. The free audit shows you the problem. The paid audit tells you exactly how to solve it.

What if I've never done any SEO?

The audit is actually more valuable if you're starting fresh, because it tells you what to build correctly from the beginning, rather than fixing what's already broken. We'll give you a clear starting point with no jargon.

What if my site is brand new?

Brand new sites can still get cited in AI answers faster than they rank on Google, especially on Perplexity and ChatGPT. The audit will show you exactly how to structure your content for maximum AI citation probability from day one.
