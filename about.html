What is AnswerRank, and why was it built?

What AnswerRank is

AnswerRank is an SEO and AEO agency built for solo founders navigating the shift from Google-only search to AI-powered discovery. We help you rank on Google and get cited in AI answers on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Mode, using one integrated strategy built specifically for people running lean.

The story

Why AnswerRank exists

In 2024, something shifted in how people find answers online.

Quietly at first, then undeniably. Millions of potential customers stopped typing into Google and started asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI instead. They stopped getting a list of links. They started getting a direct answer, one that named specific businesses, recommended specific brands, and built trust before a single click happened.

The businesses showing up in those AI answers weren't necessarily the biggest or the best-funded. They were the ones whose content was structured in a way AI systems could read, trust, and extract. The ones whose pages answered questions directly. The ones who understood the new rules.

Most solo founders hadn't figured this out. Most SEO advice was still written for a world that had already changed. And most agencies were either ignoring AI search entirely or treating it as a bolt-on to their existing Google-only workflow.

AnswerRank was built to close that gap, specifically for solo founders who move fast, run lean, and need clarity over complexity.

Not to replace SEO. SEO still matters and always will. But to build the layer the AI era demands: content that earns citations because it's genuinely structured to be found, trusted, and recommended by AI systems.

Our approach

What makes AnswerRank's approach different?

AnswerRank's methodology

AnswerRank's approach is research-backed and built for founders who don't have time for theory. Every recommendation draws on documented research, query fan-out studies, AI citation pattern analysis, Princeton's GEO research, and BrightEdge data, combined with real citation data from active client work.

We track which content structures earn citations. We monitor which schema types perform across different AI platforms. We test what actually moves the needle for lean operations, not enterprise content teams with unlimited budgets.
